Garlic Parmesan Pasta
Description:

Garlic Parmesan Pasta is a deliciously creamy and savory dish that combines the rich flavors of garlic and Parmesan cheese with a smooth, velvety sauce. This comforting pasta dish starts with garlic gently sautéed in butter, releasing its aroma and infusing the sauce with a warm, savory depth. Heavy cream is added to create a luxurious texture, followed by generous handfuls of grated Parmesan cheese, which melts into the sauce, making it rich and cheesy. The cooked pasta is tossed in this garlic-Parmesan sauce, coating each strand in creamy goodness.

Perfect as a quick weeknight dinner or an impressive side dish, Garlic Parmesan Pasta is also versatile; you can add grilled chicken, shrimp, or vegetables like spinach or broccoli for extra heartiness. The final touch of fresh parsley adds a hint of freshness and color, while a sprinkle of black pepper balances the flavors. Simple yet indulgent, this pasta is an ideal choice for anyone craving a comforting, restaurant-quality meal at home.

Ingredients:
  • 12 oz Pasta (such as spaghetti or fettuccine)
  • 4 Cloves Garlic, Minced
  • 1/4 Cup Butter
  • 1 Cup Heavy Cream
  • 1 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt & Pepper To Taste
  • Fresh Parsley, Chopped, For Garnish
Steps:
  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a large pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream, stirring until well-combined. Bring to a gentle simmer, then add the Parmesan cheese and mix until melted and smooth.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the pan, tossing to coat in the sauce. Use the reserved pasta water to thin out the sauce if necessary. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Garnish with chopped parsley and additional Parmesan if desired. Serve hot and enjoy!
